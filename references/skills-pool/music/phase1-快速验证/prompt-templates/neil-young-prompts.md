## Prompt 关键词速查

| 维度 | 关键词 |
|------|--------|
| 吉他 | acoustic strummed with pick, fingerpicking, raw distorted Les Paul |
| 口琴 | harmonica breaks between verses, neck rack harmonica |
| 人声 | high thin tenor, nasal, fragile, falsetto, raw screaming (electric) |
| 配器 | pedal steel, upright bass, brush drums, minimal rhythm section |
| 制作 | warm analog 1972, Nashville sound, live feel, one-take |
| 和弦 | I-V-vi-IV, I-IV-V, 3-4 chords maximum |
| 速度 | 75 BPM (慢歌) - 85 BPM (民谣) - 140 BPM (摇滚) |
| 结构 | verse-verse-verse, harmonica solos between verses, no bridge |
