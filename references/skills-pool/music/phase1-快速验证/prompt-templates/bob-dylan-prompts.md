## Prompt 关键词速查

| 维度 | 关键词 |
|------|--------|
| 吉他 | fingerpicked alternating bass, Travis picking, percussive strumming |
| 口琴 | harmonica solos between verses, in key of song, Hohner Marine Band |
| 人声 | nasal mid-range, conversational, rhythmic phrasing, words over melody |
| 制作 | raw, live, one-take, coffeehouse, 1963 Greenwich Village |
| 和弦 | I-IV-V, 2-3 chords only, diatonic, capo for transposition |
| 结构 | verse-verse-verse, no chorus (acoustic), verse-chorus (electric) |
| 速度 | 85 BPM (慢) - 100 BPM (中) - 115 BPM (电声) |
| 独有 | absolutely no other instruments (solo period), lyrical density is key |

## 注意事项

- Dylan 的风格核心是**歌词**，不是音乐复杂度
- AI 生成的歌词很难达到 Dylan 的文学密度，建议手动调整歌词
- 声音-guitar-harmonica 三件套是 Dylan 的标志性配置
- 可以尝试让 AI 生成 Dylan 风格的歌词框架，然后手动润色
